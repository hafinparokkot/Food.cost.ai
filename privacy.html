Privacy-First Design: FoodCost AI is built so that all food cost calculations happen entirely within your browser. The numbers you enter — ingredient costs, quantities, recipes — are never transmitted to our servers. We do not store, process, or retain your calculation data. This page explains what limited information we do collect through standard web analytics.

FoodCost AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at foodcostai.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1 Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to understand how our tools are used and to improve them. We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., via our contact form).

Analytics Data (Automatically Collected)

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic region (country/city level — not precise location)
  • Browser type, operating system, and device category
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • Interactions with calculator tools (e.g., which calculator was used) — but not the values you enter

Information You Voluntarily Provide

  • Name and email address when you submit our contact form
  • Message content you send us
✅
What we do NOT collect: We do not collect, store, or process any ingredient data, recipe data, cost figures, or any numbers you enter into our calculators. All calculations run locally in your browser and are discarded when you close the tab.

2 How We Use Information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To understand which calculators and content are most useful to our users
  • To improve the performance, design, and functionality of the Site
  • To respond to your enquiries when you contact us
  • To detect and fix technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data to build advertising profiles.

3 Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

  • Storing your dark/light mode preference (saved in localStorage)
  • Remembering any calculator preferences you set during your session

Analytics Cookies

  • Google Analytics cookies to track aggregate, anonymous usage statistics
  • These cookies do not identify you personally

Advertising Cookies

  • Google AdSense may set cookies to show relevant advertisements
  • These are controlled by Google's own privacy policy

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some Site functionality, but will not prevent you from using any of our calculators.

5 Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Our approach to data security includes:

  • All traffic to this site is served over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • All calculator computations happen locally in your browser — no calculation data is ever transmitted
  • Contact form submissions are handled securely and stored only as long as necessary to respond
  • We do not maintain databases of user calculation history

No method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we take commercially reasonable steps to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

6 Children's Privacy

FoodCost AI is designed for food business professionals and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
