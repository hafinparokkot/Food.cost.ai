How to Use the Recipe Cost Calculator

The FoodCost AI Recipe Cost Calculator helps food manufacturers, bakeries, and home food businesses calculate the exact cost of any food product. Unlike simple food cost tools, this calculator accounts for the difference between your purchase quantity and the quantity actually used in a recipe.

Why This Matters for Indian Food Businesses

You buy oil in 15L tins but use only 5L per batch. You buy sugar in 50 kg bags but use 2 kg per recipe. By entering both purchase quantity and usage quantity, we calculate your exact cost per unit used — eliminating the most common pricing error in Indian food businesses.

Food Cost Benchmarks for India

Indian food industry benchmarks suggest food cost (ingredients only) should be 28–35% of selling price for full-service restaurants, 30–40% for packaged food manufacturers, and 35–45% for cloud kitchens and delivery businesses.
