About FoodCost AI

Built for India's Food Entrepreneurs

FoodCost AI exists because too many food businesses in India fail — not because of bad food, but because of bad pricing. We're changing that with free, accessible, India-specific food costing tools.

Our Mission

Every Food Business Deserves to Know Its True Costs

India has millions of food entrepreneurs — home bakers, banana chips makers, cloud kitchen founders, traditional snack manufacturers — and most of them lose money without understanding why.

The existing tools don't work for them. Excel spreadsheets are too complex. Restaurant costing apps ignore manufacturing yield. ERP systems cost ₹50,000+ per year.

FoodCost AI fills this gap with tools specifically designed for the Indian food manufacturing context — complete with yield loss tracking, GST, distribution chain margins, and India-specific benchmarks. Free. Always.
