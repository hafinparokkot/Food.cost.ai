FoodCost AI exists because too many food businesses fail — not because of bad food, but because of bad pricing. We're changing that with free, accessible, globally-ready food costing tools built for any market, any currency.

Our Mission

Every Food Business Deserves to Know Its True Costs

Millions of food entrepreneurs globally — home bakers, snack makers, cloud kitchen founders, food manufacturers — lose money without understanding why. Bad pricing kills good food businesses.

The existing tools don't work for them. Excel spreadsheets are too complex. Restaurant costing apps ignore manufacturing yield. ERP systems cost thousands per year.

FoodCost AI fills this gap — complete with yield loss tracking, multi-currency support, configurable tax rates (GST, VAT, Sales Tax), distribution chain margins, and global benchmarks. Free. Always.
